Benefits of Stretching and General Practice for Stretching


Benefits of Stretching

1. Improves the flexibility of muscles and joints.
2. Reduces tension in muscles and joints.
3. helps the body relax or reduce body aches and pains.
4. Helps prevent injuries from fixation of ligaments, joints and muscles.
5. Helps prevent symptoms of arthritis and osteoarthritis.


General practice for Stretching

1. Stretching should be done daily or at least 3 times a week.
2. Stretching should be performed before and after every exercise.
3. Should relax the part that is being stretched.
4. Stretching should be done until the point of tension is felt. Then stand still for about 10 - 15 seconds.
5. Do not hold your breath while stretching.
6. Do not move in a jerky manner while stretching.
7. The posture for stretching the muscles in each position should be arranged correctly. To achieve the highest efficiency in stretching.

1. Side bend
2. Wing span
3. Back and forward bend
4. Neck rotation (360 degrees)
5. Neck rotation (left and right)
6. Shoulder shrug
7. Cat cow stretch
