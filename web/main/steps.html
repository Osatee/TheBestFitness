Benefits of doing "Warm-up"

1.increase heart rate to prepare to pump blood to the body Because while exercising, various organs will need more oxygen from the blood.
2.when the body begins to rise in temperature can use oxygen better
3.Nervous system communication becomes richer. make exercise more effective
4.The blood flow to the organs will increase, increasing the metabolic rate better.
5.Increase sensitivity to stretching and contracting of various muscles.
6.Helps reduce or alleviate stiffness of muscles before exercise.
7.Helps to increase the control of the rhythm of the movement of the muscles to flow more smoothly increase exercise efficiency and reduce the chance of injury
